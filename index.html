Our Story

Bridget and Brent met and formed a fast friendship, though it's unclear if it was forced due to Brent's car breaking down and their having to carpool.

They must have at least tolerated each other, though, because they ended up as housemates. Commence lots of family dinners, house cookouts, and movie nights, plus a close call with a house fire, until, in a move that apparently surprised no one, Brent and Bridget started dating.

From there followed almost immediately two years of long distance, which included lots of video calls and time zones and the occasional plane ticket, until they both ended up in the same country.

After spending the last 7 months a mere hour away from each other, they decided that they could stand to be in the same place at the same time, and Brent finally managed to surprise Bridget with the proposal (not an easy feat)!  Unfortunately Brent has been assigned to move to the other side of the country, so they'll be apart for another couple of years — but in the same time zone this time, which they consider a win.

Their love for each other and their faith has allowed them to make it through 4 moves, over 7000 miles, and 13 time zones thus far. They're excited to continue their journey together!
