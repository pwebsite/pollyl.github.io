Our Story

Bridget and Brent met in Navy flight school in Pensacola. They formed a fast friendship, though it's unclear if it was forced due to Brent's car breaking down and their having to carpool.

They must have at least tolerated each other, though, because they ended up as housemates in Corpus Christi. Commence lots of family dinners, house cookouts, and movie nights, plus a close call with a house fire, until, in a move that apparently surprised no one, Brent and Bridget started dating.

From there followed almost immediately two years of long distance — lots of video calls and time zones and the occasional plane ticket — until they both ended up in Japan! This commenced the Decide-if-they-want-to-get-married-while-they're-both-in-the-same-place Era, which was marked by road trips and Japanese convenience store snacks.

In the end, they decided to get married in order to make checking movies off the "watch together" list more convenient, and so that Bridget can mooch off Brent's body heat when she's cold (which is always).

It's already been a wild ride, and this is only the beginning! Thanks for coming to celebrate with us!
