Our Story

Mark and Kirstyn met at MIT.

The next few years were pretty tumultuous...

But when they started dating in February of 2014, they had Skype, WhatsApp, and Jesus on their side. Thirteen months later, Mark got down on one knee and asked her to join him for life as his wife.

She said yes!
